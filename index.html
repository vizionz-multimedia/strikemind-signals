Questions

Frequently Asked

What is StrikeMind Signals™? ▼
StrikeMind Signals is a probability-arbitrage intelligence service powered by three specialized AI agents — StrikeMind, Project MIA, and Striker. We don't predict price. We find where the market has mispriced probability, then only fire when all five gates pass and the score hits ≥88.
Who are the three agents? ▼
StrikeMind™ is the flagship intelligence engine — it scores every setup through 5 gates and decides when to fire. Project MIA is the market intelligence scout — pre-market alpha, macro scanning, sector rotation. Striker is the execution specialist — entry precision, stop placement, and position debrief. Together they form Trade Team Apex.
What markets does the faction cover? ▼
Equities (SPY, QQQ, sector ETFs, high-momentum single names), Cryptocurrency (BTC, ETH, SOL and major altcoins), Futures (ES, NQ, CL), and Commodities. Brief tier focuses on equities and crypto. Full Stack and FIRE add futures and deeper multi-market coverage.
How does the free trial work? ▼
Sign up with your email and Telegram username. You get 7 days of full access at your tier level — no credit card required. At the end of the trial period, you'll receive a reminder. If you choose to continue, you'll be asked to add a payment method. If not, access ends automatically. No surprise charges.
How do I receive signals after subscribing? ▼
After successful signup, you'll receive a single-use Telegram invite link via email within 24 hours. Brief tier: Telegram + WhatsApp. Full Stack: adds Slack. FIRE: adds direct Striker DMs and priority access. The invite link expires in 15 minutes — use it promptly.
What's the "five gates" system? ▼
Every signal must pass five independent validation gates before StrikeMind fires: (1) Probability Score ≥88, (2) Volume confirmation, (3) Structure alignment, (4) Macro context clear, (5) Risk/reward ratio ≥2.5:1. If any gate fails, StrikeMind stays silent. No signal is better than a bad signal.
